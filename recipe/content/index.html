How Long Does Flan Last?

Store your leftover flan in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days. You can freeze homemade flan for up to two months.

SERVINGS: 8

PREP TIME: 20 min

COOK TIME: 1 hr

CHILL TIME: aprx 2 hr or untill fully cooled

Ingredients

-1 cup white sugar
-3 large eggs
-1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
-1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
-1 tablespoon vanilla extract

ALLERGENS: milk* eggs*

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

Step 2

Melt sugar in a medium saucepan over medium-low heat until liquefied and golden in color. Carefully pour hot syrup into a 9-inch round glass baking dish, turning the dish to evenly coat the bottom and sides; set aside.

Step 3

Beat eggs in a large bowl. Add condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla; beat until smooth. Pour egg mixture into the baking dish; cover with aluminum foil.

Step 4

Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour. Let cool completely( this will take 1-2 hours.)

Step 5

Once cool, Invert onto platter: Run a sharp knife around the flan right up next to the pan, then place a large rimmed serving plate over the pan. While holding the plate in place, flip the flan to the opposite side and let if fall onto the plate.

Step 6

slice and serve cool; let caramel sauce flow over flan
